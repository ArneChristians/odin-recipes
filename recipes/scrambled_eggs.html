Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Learn how to make delicious scrambled eggs with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Optional: milk or cream for creamier texture

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and whisk until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
  5. Gently stir with a spatula, pushing the eggs from the edges to the center.
  6. Cook until the eggs are just set but still slightly runny.
  7. Remove from heat and serve immediately.

Enjoy your scrambled eggs!
