Steak Recipe

←

Description

Learn how to cook a perfect steak with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak (your choice of cut)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil or butter for cooking
  • Optional: garlic and herbs for flavor

Steps

  1. Take the steak out of the fridge and let it come to room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Season both sides of the steak generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill over high heat and add olive oil or butter.
  4. Once the oil is hot, place the steak in the skillet or on the grill.
  5. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, adjusting time based on thickness and desired doneness.
  6. If using, add garlic and herbs during the last minute of cooking for extra flavor.
  7. Remove the steak from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  8. Slice against the grain and serve.

Enjoy your delicious steak!
