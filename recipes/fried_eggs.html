Fried Eggs Recipe

←

Description

Learn how to make perfect fried eggs with this simple recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Optional: herbs or cheese for extra flavor

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  2. Crack the eggs into the skillet, being careful not to break the yolks.
  3. Cook for about 2-3 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. If desired, sprinkle with herbs or cheese before serving.

Enjoy your fried eggs!
